ABOUT US

The Qatar University ACM Club is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing computer science and technology education. Our mission is to foster a vibrant community of students passionate about technology. We organize a wide range of events, including workshops, seminars, and hackathons, aimed at providing hands-on experience and networking opportunities for our members.
